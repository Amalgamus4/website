Background

I started my journey to become a self taught software developer in early 2021. I had previously worked a multitude of varying jobs from restaurants, to manufacturing, to welding, to machine maintenance. Nothing I had worked at was really something I felt I wanted to do the rest of my life, however. I'm not sure what made me decided to become a software developer, but once I made up my mind nothing could change it. I learned everything I could as quickly as possible, which isn't easy when balancing a full time job and a family. Now that I have a decent knowledge of the necessary languages, I am ready to begin my career as a software developer.

I have been happily married for almost 5 years and have two children, two cats, and a dog.
