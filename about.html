Background

I had previously worked a multitude of varying jobs from restaurants, to manufacturing, to welding, to machine maintenance. Nothing I had worked at was really something I felt I wanted to do the rest of my life, however. I'm not sure what made me decided to become a software developer, but once I made up my mind nothing could change it. I am learning as much as I can as quickly as possible and hope to be ready to start applying for jobs within the next year.

I have been happily married for almost 5 years to an amazing woman and have two children, two cats, and a dog.
